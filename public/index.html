Welcome!

Children's Hope says hello to everybody!. We are a care center devoted to support children or young adults with disabilities, and their families. Our association has multiples centers around Italy. You will be able to find some services in each one of them.
All children have the right to quality and free education, but millions of them are excluded because of poverty, gender, disability, geographical distance, language or cultural barriers.
Now is your chance to help them!
